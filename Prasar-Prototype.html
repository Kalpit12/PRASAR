PRASAR

Digital Outreach with a Devotional Heart
PersonalizedReliableAuthenticSeamlessAccessibleRadiant

Connecting the Global Satsang through Divine Communication for PR. PRASAR is designed to serve dignified, timely, and heartfelt communication workflows for Karyakars and administrators across regions.

P — Personalized

The touch of individual care in every message.

Every interaction is unique.

PRASAR automates high-quality, formatted invitation workflows that address every recipient by name. The result feels like a personal invitation from the Sanstha, not a generic broadcast.

R — Reliable

Dependable delivery for every Utsav and update.

Timing with consistency.

For last-minute Sabha updates or large festival outreach, PRASAR supports dependable dispatch via Email and WhatsApp with operational consistency across Africa and beyond.

A — Authentic

The official voice of the Sanstha.

Single source of truth.

Every communication carries official BAPS branding and standardized templates, helping recipients trust that information is verified, accurate, and aligned with the Sanstha's identity.

S — Seamless

Technology that works in the background.

Focus on Seva, not tools.

PDF generation, contact handling, event-linked invitation flows, and communication records are unified into one smooth experience so Karyakars can execute PR tasks in a few guided steps.

A — Accessible

Bridging generations and geography.

Built for everyone.

From elders to youth, PRASAR is designed to be simple and mobile-friendly, ensuring updates and invitations are accessible to every outreach contact regardless of location or device.

R — Radiant

Spreading the light of Satsang to every corner.

Communication with spiritual purpose.

True to its name, PRASAR exists to spread the glory of Akshar-Purushottam Maharaj and the Satpurush, making each communication a reflection of peace, beauty, and wisdom.

One Platform, One Devotional Standard

PRASAR unites personalization, trust, and operational excellence so every invitation, update, and follow-up remains spiritually aligned and professionally delivered. It is not only a communication system — it is an extension of Seva in the digital era.
